
# Linaz Protocol

### The first network where you tell it what you want to happen with your money — and it makes it happen completely and safely, or not at all.

---

## The Problem

Every day a nurse in Manila sends money home to her children. She walks into a Western Union and hands over $300 — two weeks of 12 hour shifts. $35 disappears in fees before it crosses a single border. That is four hours of her life. Taken. Before her mother sees a cent.

She heard crypto was different. Borderless. Instant. Free. She tried it. One wrong character in a 42 digit address and $300 was gone in four seconds. No reversal. No recourse. No one to call.

She went back to Western Union.

She is not one woman. She is 800 million people moving $700 billion dollars across borders every year. $42 billion of it evaporates in fees. The ones who tried crypto lost even more.

Crypto failed them. Not because the technology was wrong. Because nobody finished building it.

Every protocol ever built tells you how to move money. Not one of them guarantees where it arrives. Not one of them gives you your money back if something goes wrong. Not one of them was built for someone who has never heard the word blockchain.

---

## The Solution

Linaz is an intent and outcome execution network.

You do not interact with protocols. You do not set gas. You do not select chains. You do not manage wallet addresses.

You just say what you want:

*"Send $300 to Mom. Keep it safe. Bring it back if she doesn't confirm."*

Linaz reads that intent. Compiles it into a structured execution plan. A network of specialized solvers — think of them like racers competing to find the fastest safest cheapest path — picks it up in milliseconds. The winner executes it atomically. Either every single step completes perfectly and safely or the entire thing reverses completely. There is no state where you lose money to a mistake.

Value on Linaz knows where it is going. Why it is going there. What happens if anything goes wrong. And how to come home if it does.

---

## How It Works

**Intent Layer**
Users express goals in plain language. A natural language compiler translates them into structured intents with hard constraints and soft objectives. Constraints define what must be true. Objectives define what must be optimized. Solvers must satisfy all constraints before competing on objective quality.

**Solver Network**
Solvers are specialized automated systems that compete to execute intents. Like Bitcoin miners but for outcomes. They pre-build execution paths for every intent type. When an intent is broadcast the fastest valid solver wins. Every execution is wrapped atomically — 100% successful or 100% reversed.

**Programmable Asset Layer**
The ecosystem token is not inert. It has behavior baked into its fundamental design. It knows its purpose. It enforces its own conditions. It can reverse itself. It burns on one chain and mints on another without a bridge. Value that knows what it is supposed to do.

**Truth and Proof System**
Every condition, every outcome, every state transition requires proof. On-chain proof. ZK proof. Oracle consensus. Institutional attestation. Nothing is assumed. Everything is verified. The entire system rests on one principle — truth and proof. If that collapses everything collapses. It won't.

## Principles

**Truth and Proof** — every claim must be verifiable. Every condition must be proven. The system makes lying maximally expensive not just technically difficult.

**Outcomes not instructions** — users express what they want. The network figures out how. Complexity lives at the infrastructure layer not the user layer.

**Atomic or nothing** — every execution completes completely or reverses completely. There is no state where a user loses money to a mistake or a failure.

**Value with agency** — assets are not inert. They know their purpose, enforce their conditions, and protect themselves. Money that knows what it is supposed to do.
